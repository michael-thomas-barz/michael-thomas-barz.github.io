I am a first year mathematics PhD student at Princeton University. My graduate studies are supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship program. Thanks NSF!

I did my undergraduate studies at the University of Chicago. At Chicago, Matt Emerton and Sasha Beilinson very generously taught me most of the math I know. I will forever be grateful to them.

My research interests are in algebraic geometry, number theory, and singularities.

My favorite books are Sally's Fundamentals of Mathematical Analysis, The God of Small Things by Arundhati Roy, and The Dream of Scipio by Iain Pears (not the one by Cicero!); and I also love the music of Phillip Glass.
